about me

My name is Rene Vasquez. For the past 6 months I have begun a journey to learn a new set of skills that many people consider to be daunting: I want to learn to code. I left school in 2021 after being disillusioned with the thought of getting a degree with a major in government. Leaving school has been the biggest risk I have taken in my life. Leaving the traditional educational pipeline is becoming less and less unconventional, but the stigma still lingers. I chose to attend a three-month long coding boot camp in hopes of becoming a full-stack developer. I enjoyed nearly every second of my classes, but found it difficult to absorb all the information, so I created this website to catalogue my journey of learning web development. I hope you enjoy this website, and hopefully you might learn something as well.
